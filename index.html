Welcome

Welcome to the Horndean Youth Round Robin Tournament 2025! We're delighted to welcome six fantastic teams to compete in two exciting groups.

Each team will play the others in their group twice. The winners of each group will go head-to-head in the final to be crowned champions.

Thank you to all players, coaches, and parents formaking this possible - enjoy the day, play with passion, and show great sportsmanship!

Rules

  • 7-a-side with rolling substitutions
  • Matches are 20 minutes long
  • Each team plays their group opponents twice
  • 3 points for a win, 1 for a draw, 0 for a loss
  • Group winners progress to the final
  • Tie-breakers: goal difference, goals scored, head-to-head
  • Respect everyone on and off the pitch - enjoy the football!
